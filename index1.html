CASE STUDY

The NADIS Predictive Platform

The Caribbean loses $275 Million USD a year in damages and business lost due to environmental disturbances such as the presence of sargassum seaweed and unexpected droughts. Coastal properties that rely on beaches and coastal features to operate are affects the most by environmental disturbances.

NADIS tracks weather and climate conditions to make predictions on environmental disturbances. This data is used to sell clean beaches and consistent atmospheres that are crucial to the $7.6 Billion USD tourism industry. The company is aimed at creating a proactive approach to disturbances, rather than reactive.

NADIS Is powered by Project Jaguar, and became the winner of DadliHack 2.0 in Antigua, West Indies in 2019. NADIS is now working together with the UNOPS, Government of Antigua and Barbuda and Ocean Generation to bring the award winning solution around the world.
